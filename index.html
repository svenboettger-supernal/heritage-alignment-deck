Reading the comparison

These are genuinely different products. The initial quote was a thin coordination layer over the existing tools. The new build is a full operating system that captures meetings, writes the letters, answers by chat and voice, and runs the task workspace. The price reflects that broader scope, not a change in rate. The one trade-off worth discussing is the change-control gate Heritage asked for, which the new scope no longer enforces by default.
